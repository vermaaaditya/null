Competition Guidelines

  • Participants must carry their own laptops for the event.
  • Limited internet usage will be permitted during the competition.
  • Students can participate individually or as part of a team.
  • Participants must develop a fully functional website/web app based on the given theme.
  • Final submissions must include:
    Complete source code.
    Project documentation, details.
  • Original Work Only:
    Plagiarism is strictly prohibited.
    Pre-built or previously developed projects will not be accepted.
  • Coding must begin only after the official start of the competition.
  • All participants must adhere to the rules and guidance provided by the coordinators.
  • You are free to use any technology stack of your choice.
  • Preferred stacks: MERN, MEAN, PERN, T3 Stack.

Marking Scheme (100 Marks)

Idea & Innovation – 15

Relevance to Theme – 10

Approach & Problem Solving – 15

Functionality & Performance – 20

UI/UX Design – 15

Code Quality – 15

Presentation – 10
